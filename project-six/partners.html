PARTNERS

The Nation’s Capital Hoop Classic will showcase your products and services across diverse media platforms or integrated into the game experience. This event can help you gain visibility and enhance your marketing effort that allows our partners to reach a different demographic while also benefiting from radio, print, and digital advertising. Sponsors will work with us to coordinate the implementation and activation of the partnership and make it as turnkey as possible. Sponsors can create a customized program using a “menu” approach that will be priced according to what is included. Our goal is to create a meaningful package that achieves your marketing objectives and adheres to your budget. Sponsors can get involved on a local, regional or national basis.

    Benefits of partnership

  • Ideal marketing platform
  • Maximize brand awareness & exposure
  • Dedicated to helping you reach your goals & objectives
  • Entitlement
  • Category Exclusivity
  • Courtside Signage
  • Customized Marketing Promotions
  • On-Court Promotions & Presentations
  • On-Site Display Area
  • Public Address Announcements
  • Souvenir Game Program Advertisements
  • Premium Tickets and VIP Hospitality
  • Entitlement of a Post-Game Award
  • Recognition & Link on Event Web Site
